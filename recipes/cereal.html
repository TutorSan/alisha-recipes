Alisha's Homemade Cereal

Description

The critically acclaimed cereal recipe from the world famous Chef Alisha. This recipe will blow your socks away!

Ingredients

  • Kellogg's Cornflakes
  • Milk of your choice; Milk, Almond milk, or Oat milk
  • Strong Ceramic bowl
  • Spoon

Steps

  1. Add Kellogg's cornflakes to bowl
  2. Add milk of your choice to the bowl
  3. Insert spoon into bowl
  4. Grasp onto spoon, scoop up a mouthful of cereal and enjoy!
